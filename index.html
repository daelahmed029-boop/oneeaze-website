When will OneEaze be available?

Yes! The OneEaze app is completely free to download and use. We make money through small commissions from our partner merchants and premium features for businesses.

How is my data protected?

We use bank-level encryption and follow strict data protection protocols. Your personal and financial information is always secure and never shared with third parties without your consent.

Which cities will you launch in first?

We're starting with Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, then expanding to other major cities based on waitlist demand from each location.
